Process

From a question to a published note.

Step 01

Screen

Quantitative and qualitative filters narrow the universe to names that warrant deeper work.

Step 02

Read

Annual reports, regulatory filings, conference calls, and competitor disclosures, read in full, not skimmed.

Step 03

Model

Forecasts, valuation, and scenarios are built bottom-up. Every assumption is flagged and stressed.

Step 04

Disclose

The note is published with a complete disclosure pack, conflicts, holdings, methodology, and risks.
