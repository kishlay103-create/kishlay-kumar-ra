Investor Charter for Research Analysts

As prescribed by SEBI vide circular SEBI/HO/IMD/IMD-II CIS/P/CIR/2021/0685 dated December 13, 2021 (and subsequent updates).

A. Vision

To provide quality, unbiased, independent and research-driven information to investors so as to enable them to take informed investment decisions.

B. Mission

  • To provide high-quality, research-based information and analysis.
  • To act with integrity, transparency and fairness in all dealings with clients.
  • To uphold the principles of disclosure and avoid any conflict of interest.

C. Services Provided to Investors

  • Issuance of research reports based on analysis of listed securities.
  • Issuance of trading calls based on technical / fundamental analysis.
  • Periodic updates and follow-ups on research reports issued.

D. Rights of Investors

  • Right to receive research reports based on independent and unbiased analysis.
  • Right to seek clarifications on the research reports issued.
  • Right to confidentiality of personal information.
  • Right to grievance redressal as per SEBI prescribed mechanism.

E. Expectations from Investors (Responsibilities)

  • Deal with SEBI registered Research Analysts only.
  • Pay fees only through banking channels and obtain a receipt.
  • Read all research reports / disclaimers carefully before taking any investment decision.
  • Provide complete and accurate information to the Research Analyst.
  • Do not share trading credentials or OTPs with any person.

F. Grievance Redressal Mechanism

Investors can lodge complaints / grievances against the Research Analyst as per the procedure described in the Grievance Redressal section below.

Do's and Don'ts for Investors

Do's

  • Always deal with SEBI registered Research Analysts.
  • Check for SEBI registration number on the website and reports.
  • Pay only through legitimate banking channels.
  • Always ask for and read the disclosures and disclaimers.

Don'ts

  • Do not provide funds for investment to the Research Analyst.
  • Do not fall prey to assured / guaranteed return schemes.
  • Do not share login credentials, OTP, PIN or any account information.
  • Do not deal with unregistered entities or social-media tipsters.

Disclosure under SEBI (Research Analysts) Regulations, 2014

  • The Research Analyst is registered with SEBI as an Individual Research Analyst (Reg. No. INH000022826).
  • The Research Analyst, his relatives, or his associates do not have any financial interest, beneficial ownership of 1% or more, or any other material conflict of interest in the subject companies covered, except as may be expressly disclosed in the relevant research report.
  • The Research Analyst has not received any compensation from the subject companies in the past twelve months.
  • The Research Analyst has not managed or co-managed the public offering of any subject company.
  • The Research Analyst has not served as an officer, director or employee of any subject company.
  • The Research Analyst has not been engaged in market making activity for any subject company.
