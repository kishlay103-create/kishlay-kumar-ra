Philosophy

Why this practice exists, and what it refuses to do.

What I do

Publish research. Disclose everything.

  • ✓Long-form equity research with stated thesis, valuation, and risks.
  • ✓Public disclosure of holdings, conflicts, and methodology.
  • ✓A documented complaints process and an Investor Charter.
  • ✓Communications and advice strictly within SEBI's RA framework.
What I do not do

No tips. No execution. No commissions.

  • ×No "stock tips," intraday calls, or guaranteed-return schemes.
  • ×No portfolio management or distribution of mutual funds / insurance.
  • ×No execution of trades on behalf of clients.
  • ×No paid promotions, paid coverage, or undisclosed compensation.

A note on independence

This is a single-analyst practice. Independence is structural, not aspirational, there are no execution incentives, no AUM-linked fees, and no third-party arrangements that could colour a published view.
